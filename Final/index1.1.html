The game of Tetris

Tetris is a game that many people over the years have enjoyed playing. It is a game where shapes fall down from the sky, and you as an invisible character that can control these shapes have to place them in an unbroken line. In some regards, it is a lot of responsibility; but in other regards, it is a very peaceful game. Unfortunately, there is no way to win the game, and users are forced at some point in time to have to lose, no matter how good at the game they may be.
